# Utilization Management Operational Guidance

## Prior Authorization Turnaround Times

### Standard Requests
- Determination within **14 calendar days** of receipt

### Urgent Requests
Requests may be processed as **urgent** when delay could seriously jeopardize the member’s health.

Urgent turnaround:
- Determination within **72 hours**

## Continuity-of-Care Handling
When a member is transitioning coverage and is receiving an ongoing course of treatment:

- Requests may qualify for continuity-of-care consideration
