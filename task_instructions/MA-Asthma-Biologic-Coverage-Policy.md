# Medical Policy: Biologic Therapy for Severe Asthma (Medicare Advantage)

## Overview
This policy defines medical necessity and coverage requirements for biologic therapies indicated for the treatment of severe asthma in Medicare Advantage members.

## Medical Necessity Criteria
Biologic therapy may be considered medically necessary when **all** of the following criteria are met:

1. Member has a confirmed diagnosis of **severe persistent asthma**
2. Symptoms remain uncontrolled despite:
   - High-dose inhaled corticosteroids AND
   - Long-acting beta-agonist therapy
3. Evidence of frequent exacerbations (≥2 in the prior 12 months)
4. Prescribing provider is a pulmonologist or allergist

## Step Therapy Requirements
Step therapy **is required** prior to approval of biologic therapy unless an exception applies.

Required steps:
- Trial of high-dose inhaled corticosteroids
- Trial of at least one additional controller medication

## Prior Authorization
**Prior authorization is required** for all biologic therapies addressed under this policy.
